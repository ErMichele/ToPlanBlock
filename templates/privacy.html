Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 13, 2026

1. Data Controller

The data controller for this application is ToPlanBlock. For any privacy-related inquiries, you can contact: micheleacciuffi@gmail.com.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account Information: Email and Username required for registration and authentication.
  • User Content: Tasks and Categories created within the application.
  • Profile Imagery: Optional profile pictures uploaded by the user.

To provide our services, we share specific data with the following processors:

4. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time via the "Account Settings" page. Deleting your account will result in the permanent removal of:

  • Your profile information and credentials.
  • All associated tasks and categories.
  • Your profile image (it will be programmatically removed from our storage and Cloudinary).
